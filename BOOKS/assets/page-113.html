Pakalolo-tato Eggplant Marsala
*45 minutes
3 Japanese eggplants, cut in -inch slices
3 potatoes, peeled and diced in 1-inch pieces
1 large carrot, cut into -inch slices
cup Midnight Oil
4 cups veggie stock
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp salt
1) Peel and cut potatoes and carrots. Place in medium
saucepan with veggie stock. Cook until tender, 10C15
minutes.
2) Bust out the hubbly bubbly, load up the party bowl,
and everybody grab a hose.
3) Heat Midnight Oil in large saucepan and lightly fry
eggplant. Add all spices, and simmer for 3C5 minutes.
4) Add cooked carrots, potatoes, and veggie stock to
eggplant mixture.
5) Simmer on medium heat until sauce thickens, about
15C20 minutes, allowing your saliva glands to acti-
vate.
6) Serve hot over rice or with bread.
