Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Mind-Melt Mint Chocolate Brownies
*45 minutes
1 cup cocoa powder
cup butter
cup Butter-Ganj
2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp mint extract
1 cup flour
2 cups fresh berries
1) Pre-bake yourself a full 360 degrees. Preheat your
oven to 325.
2) In medium saucepan or in a microwave-safe bowl,
melt Butter-Ganj, then whisk in cocoa powder and sug-
ar.
3) Add eggs, vanilla, and mint extract. Mix until smooth.
4) Add flour and stir until wet, but dont overmix or
your delicate brownies will be chewy.
5) Spread in greased 13x9 pan or pizza pan for fun pre-
sentation. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
6) Serve with Uncle Johns Chocolate Syrup (below).
Note: Do not overbake. (I personally like them
cooked so theyre kind of fudgy, but it s up to you.)
