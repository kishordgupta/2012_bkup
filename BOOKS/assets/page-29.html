1) Cut the cheese (tee-hee) into 3x1 inch sticks.
2) Whisk together eggs, Midnight Oil, and milk.
3) Combine bread crumbs and seasonings in a separate
bowl.
4) Dip sticks in egg mixture, then generously coat with
bread crumbs. You can repeat this process if you want
thicker breading.
5) Place sticks on a cookie sheet and freeze until solid.
Note: This is an important step because if its not
frozen, the cheese wants to melt before it is golden
brown, and then you have a ball of melted fried goo.
(Its still yummy, but it doesnt look good.)
6) While the sticks are chillin, grab your piece and get
your fry on.
7) Preheat frying oil to 350.
8) Place 6 to 8 sticks in oil and fry for 3 to 5 minutes
or until golden brown. Drain on a paper towel.
Hint: After sticks are frozen solid, you dont have to
use them right awayyou can put them in an air-
tight container in the freezer and have them ready
for the next munchie attack.
