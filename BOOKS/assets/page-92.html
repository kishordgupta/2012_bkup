Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) For practice sake, take this time to spin up a J and
put yourself in the mood to play.
2) Heat wok or large skillet with cup Midnight Oil.
3) Add cabbage, onion, carrots, and bean sprouts. Give
it a toss and cook for 5C7 minutes.
4) Add soy sauce and seasonings. Cook for another 2
minutes. (Is your mouth watering yet?)
5) Remove from wok and place on a cookie sheet, al-
lowing to cool.
6) While cooling, combine cornstarch and water.
7) Place 1 egg roll wrapper on flat, dry surface, with
one corner facing you. Use half a handful of cabbage
mixture, and place in the middle. Follow instructions
for forming egg roll found on the wrapper package,
and seal with water and cornstarch solution. (Dont get
scared; it seems more complicated than it is.)
8) Heat frying oil to 350. Place 4C6 rolls in pan at a
time, and fry 4C7 minutes or until golden brown.
9) Remove, and enjoy hot, with Sweet n Twisted
Sauce (see page 92).
Hint: Egg rolls can be made ahead of time and kept
frozen in a Ziploc bag. The next time you get am-
bushed by the munchies, all you have to do is reach
into the freezer and get your fry on . You could also
make the Sweet n Twisted Sauce in advance, and keep
it in a jar in the fridge. One, twoinstant party!
