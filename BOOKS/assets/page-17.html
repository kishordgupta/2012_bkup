Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Marijuana Cooking 101
As a true connoisseur of the cannabis plant, I recognize that every
strain has its attributes, and through the cooking process, those ef-
fects are heightened and transferred.
What is Butter-Ganj?
Butter-Ganj is the fat that rises to the top after infusing THC from
the cannabis plant into butter.
What is Midnight-
Oil?
Midnight Oil results when THC from the cannabis plant is infused
into any type of oil, such as olive, peanut, salad, or fry oil.
What is the difference between Butter-Ganj and Mid-
night-oil?
Butter-Ganj should be used when you desire a buttery flavor and
texture. If you are using it for stovetop recipes, keep in mind not
to burn it.
Midnight Oil can be used for everything else. I have baked it, fried it,
saut
ed it, had it on a salad, and put it on toast. The only thing to
keep in mind is not to heat it beyond the burning point of the base
oil.
What is Jungle Juice?
Jungle juice is cannabis that has been soaked in at least 45-proof
alcohol for 10 days to 6 months.
