Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Whisk together pudding mix, OJ, jungle juice and
sour cream. Refrigerate while you cut up the bananas
and strawberries.
2) Take a glass cake pan and line the bottom with
crepes. Spread 1 cup pudding on the crepes and deco-
rate with fruit, then add 1 cup of Ganja-Whip and
spread it out. Add another layer of crepes, and repeat
the process with the filling a second time.
3) You are now ready to take breakfast to a new level.
