Uncle Bobs Breakfast Burritos
*20 minutes
4C6 10-inch flour tortillas
8C10 eggs, beaten
1 cup broccoli florets
cup onion, diced
cup red peppers, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
cup Midnight Oil
1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
tsp salt
tsp pepper
1) My uncle Bob only wakes up one waywith a spliff
and a smile. So follow his lead and lift your spirits.
2) In a large skillet, heat Midnight Oil, add garlic, veg-
gies, salt, and pepper. Cook for 4C6 minutes.
3) Add eggs; cook for 8C10 minutes, giving it a good
stir every few minutes to prevent burning. Remove
from heat when dry.
4) Warm up tortillas, scoop in eggs, sprinkle with
cheese, and roll up that baby like you are Bob himself.
5) Serve with salsa, and chow down.
