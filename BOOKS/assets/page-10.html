Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Introduction
To the lovers of all things green,
I have been blessed with a vision by Ganjadite
[gahn-ja-dye-tee] herself. I have been charged to
create a marijuana cookbook that gives the world
an easy to follow, fun to read, cool to look at
cookbook, one that shows how to make absolutely
divine food that will truly elevate your perspec-
tive. I began to realize that dinner parties allowed
people to relax enough to express their unique-
ness, to connect to one another, and to connect to
the vastness of themselves. Most of all, I felt a real
sense of community and family. I have always be-
lieved that we nurture our spirits through sharing
food. And in todays culture, we have lost pieces
of that spirit by not eating together. I thought,
Wouldnt it be great if everyone could create this
magic in his or her own kitchen?
So off to work I went. I began to throw regional
dinner parties to try out the recipes. I was not
prepared for the gifts that came flowing back.

people, they responded by contributing their light
and beauty through suggestions. Every week held
a new piece, and all the right people came to lend
