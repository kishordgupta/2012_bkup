Holy Bowley Chicken Mole
*1 hour
2 lb chicken thighs
cup Midnight Oil
24 oz can tomato sauce
cup cocoa powder
cup chili powder
2 Tbsp cumin
2 Tbsp sugar
1 chipotle pepper, minced
1 tsp salt
cayenne to taste
1) Allow this dish to transport you deep into the heart
of Mexico. Roll a fatty and put on your sombrerowel-
come to your personal fiesta.
2) Put chicken thighs in 9x12 pan and bake for 15
minutes.
3) Combine the rest of the ingredients in a saucepan
and bring to a simmer. Pull out chicken and cover with
sauce.
4) Bake uncovered at 325 for 35C40 minutes.
5) Serve hot over rice or just by itself.
