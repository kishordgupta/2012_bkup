Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Melt butter in crockpot on low setting.
2) Add weed, making sure all plant material is covered in butter.
3) Cover, and let that baby go for at least 12 hours.
4) Pour butter through the coffee filter, allowing container to catch
the liquid.
5) Reserve the leaf for a batch of Pot-sto (see page 25).
6) Freeze container of butter until it hardens.
7) Remove fat and put into an airtight container.
8) Keep in freezer until needed.
Stovetop Method
Important: The trick to this method is that you have
to keep putting water in the bottom pot if you cook it
more than three hours. If you space on this important
step, you could ruin the entire project.
Hint: Some say to grind up the weed into a powder,
and others think that it doesnt matter, as long as
you cook it for more than twelve hours. My opinion
is that if you are not going to be able to cook it for
twelve hours, then you should totally grind it, but if
you have the time, my preferred method is to add the
leaf as is, so I can make Pot-sto with it later.
