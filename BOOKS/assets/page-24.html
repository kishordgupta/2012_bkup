Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Stovetop Method
Materials needed:
doubleboiler
water
2cupsoilofyourchoice
weed(seechart,pg9)
coffeefilters
funnel
aglassjarthathasanairtightlid
1) Put water in bottom of double boiler, and turn on low heat.
Place second pot on top.
2) Pour in oil, add pot, submerge, and cover. Allow to cook for as
long as possible but no more than 22 hours.
3) Allow the oil to cool down a bit so the heat doesnt crack the
glass jar. Put coffee filter into the funnel; funnel on top of jar.
Pour in the oil.
4) Reserve the leaf for a batch of Pot-sto. Seal jar and keep in a
dark space.
Note: I dont recommend storing it in the fridge be-
cause of the exposure to light every time the door is
openedmaybe use a dark corner of the pantry.
