Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Smashed Potatoes
*25 minutes
3¨C4 lb potatoes
cup Butter-Ganj
cup sour cream
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
tsp pepper
1) Wash and cut potatoes. Place in cooking pot.
2) Cover with water and cook potatoes for 20 minutes
or until tender.
3) Place pot in pipe and smoke.
4) Drain water off, and put potatoes back into pot.
5) Add Butter-Ganj, sour cream, and seasonings. Mix
until creamy.
Warning: This dish could lead to sedation because
you just want to eat it all .
