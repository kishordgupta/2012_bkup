Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Green Fried Tomatoes with a Ragin
Cajun Dip
*20 minutes total
Green Fried Tomatoes *15 minutes
3 lb green tomatoes, sliced -inch thick
3 cups corn meal
1 cup flour
4 eggs
cup milk
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
cup Midnight Oil
2 cups frying oil
Hint: This is a quick munchie fix if you have green
tomatoes, but to be honest, once you are fried, you
can fry just about anything and get the same effect,
doncha think?
1) Slice tomatoes.
2) Mix cornmeal, flour, salt, and pepper.
3) In a separate container, whisk together eggs, Mid-
night Oil, and milk.
4) Heat skillet with frying oil until smoking.
5) Dip tomatoes in egg mixture, then cover with corn-
meal. For thicker breading, repeat dipping process.
