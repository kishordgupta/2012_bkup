Materials needed:
largeMasonjarwithlid
1liter45prooforhigheralcohol
weed(seechart,pg9)

darkglassbottlewithlid
1) Grind weed into a fine powder and put into Mason jar. Pour in
liquor (save a few swigs for yourself). Screw on lid and get your
shake on.
2) Set the jar aside; shake every few hours.
Note: Heres the tricky part. Some say that you will have jungle
juice in 12 hours, and thats true, but the longer you can let it
soak, the better. I usually let it go at least three months and then
repeat the process with the same weed. I know this seems excessive,
but you will really catch a buzz off the second batch.
3) Put the coffee filter into the funnel, the funnel in the dark
bottle. Pour liquor through filter. Cap bottle and store in cool,
dark space.
