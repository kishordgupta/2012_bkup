Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Olive Utopinade with Pita
*8 minutes
8 oz kalamata olives
8 oz garlic-stuffed green olives
8 oz black olives
8 oz pine nuts
cup Midnight Oil
2 tsp black pepper
tsp cayenne
1 package of pita bread
1) Preheat the oven to 325.
2) Pluck a bud from the righteously green bush, and
step through the gates of space and time into perfec-
tion.
3) Put everything into the food processor, and fire
that baby up. Let it go for 2 minutes. The consistency
should be finely diced or minced.
4) Cut pita bread into triangles; lay out flat on sheet
pan; brush tops with Midnight Oil.
5) Bake at 325 for 7C8 minutes.
6) Serve cold with pita. Garnish with basil sprigs.
Hint: Once you taste this, you, too, will want a con-
stant stash of this Mediterranean delight.
Warning: It is really easy to overeat your way into a
lapse of space and time, so be careful .
