Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
cup honey
tsp coriander
tsp cumin
tsp garam masala (this is an Indian spice)
tsp cardamom
tsp salt
tsp cinnamon
tsp cayenne pepper
1) Set the mood by lighting up the magic lamp.
2) Combine Midnight Oil and spices in a medium
saucepan. Bring to a simmer.
3) Roughly grind the fungus and add to the magic pot.
4) Add dried fruit, apple cider vinegar, and honey.
Continue to cook until liquid is gone, 10C15 minutes.
5) Allow to cool and store for at least 24 hours. The
flavors need time to build their complex structure.
6) Serve at room temperature with bread, crackers, or
whatever.
Hint: I like to have a jar of this stony deliciousness
in my fridge at all times. It s a great accompaniment
to everything.
