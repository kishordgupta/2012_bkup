Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Megs Bud-a-rific Sweet Potato
Hash Browns
*30 minutes
4C6 cups sweet potatoes, peeled and diced small
1 cup onion, diced small
cup Midnight Oil
tsp salt
tsp pepper
tsp cinnamon
tsp nutmeg
tsp cayenne
1) Spark up a morning J, and do this diner-style.
2) Heat Midnight Oil in a cast-iron skillet. When you
flick a few drops of water in the pan and it pops, the
skillet is hot enough. Reduce heat to medium.
3) Add potatoes and onion. Give it a good stir, then
cover. Allow to cook undisturbed for 3C5 minutes.
4) Uncover, add spices, stir, cover. Allow to cook for an-
other 3C5 minutes.
5) Keep turning the potatoes every 3C5 minutes, until
they are perfectly done. Roughly about 20C25 minutes
total.
6) Serve hot, dusted with cinnamon.
Hint: This is good any time with just about anything,
but my favorite is with Blissfully Baked Biscuits and
Wavy Gravy. Makes for a super-silly Sunday.
