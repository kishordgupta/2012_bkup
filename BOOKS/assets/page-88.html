Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Cake *50 minutes
cup butter
cup Butter-Ganj
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 cups water
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
tsp salt
1) Preheat your oven to 325.
2) Combine frozen peaches, Midnight Oil, sugar and
cinnamon in large skillet. Cook for 8C10 minutes on
medium heat. Remove from heat and set aside. (It is
very temping just to eat the peaches, but try to save
them for the cake.)
3) Mix together Butter-Ganj, butter, and sugar. Add
eggs and vanilla. Mix until creamy.
4) Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt to
egg mixture. Mix until lump-free.
5) Pour batter into 13x9 cake pan, saving the bowl
and spoon for messy fun while you wait for the cake to
bake.
6) Spoon peach mixture over top of batter. (Save the
bowl for licking pleasure.) Bake for 35C40 minutes.
