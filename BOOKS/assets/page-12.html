Thank You!
Is it possible to mention the hundreds of beauti-
ful people who have contributed their love and
inspiration to the forming of this book? Not
without putting you to sleep. So I will try to
keep it manageable while mentioning as many
pivotal people as possible. (Say that ten times,
fast.) First, I must thank Ganjadite for her
blessing and inspiration. She has paved the way
in a golden green. Then, thanks to Jenn, who
triggered the angles. Next, to Meg who showed
me I can accomplish anything; to my dear
friends, Pax and Coyote for their relentless
support; and most of all to my awesome mother
for giving me the gift of love.
The art in this book is phenomenal, and the
artists who created it are rich in character and
talent. Thank you, Flame, Sage, Stephanie, Rie,
Cloud, John, Harley, Memory, Kyle and Deborah.
You have deepened the texture of our culture
and encouraged us to lighten up a bit. Heres
the part when I could go on forever connecting
the hundredsin fact, thousandsof rainbow-
colored threads that created this fantastic tap-
estry of the marijuana community. You, too,
have joined our ranks to celebrate life and the
joy that connects us.
So, thank you, everyone.
