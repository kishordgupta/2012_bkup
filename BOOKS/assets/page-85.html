Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Heat up the ganja skillet, crack your brain, and get
fried.
2) Now heat oil in a cast-iron skillet to crackling hot.
3) Combine flour, salt, and pepper in medium mixing
bowl.
4) Frantically whisk together eggs, milk and Midnight
Oil in medium mixing bowl.
5) Cut steaks into 6-oz pieces, and dip into egg mix-
ture; then cover in flour on both sides. (Its best to
use one hand for wet stuff and the other hand for the
flour.)
6) Repeat egg-dip/flour process.
7) Place cutlets in hot frying pan and cook 4C6 min-
utes or until golden brown on both sides.
8) Remove, and place on paper towels.
Hint: You really cant go wrong with fried meat.
Wavy Gravy *15 minutes
2 oz Midnight Oil
4 oz flour
3 cups milk
1 willing friend
salt and pepper to taste
