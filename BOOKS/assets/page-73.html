Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
All-Lit-Up Veggie Kabobs with
Get-Roasted Garlic Tahini Nut Sauce
*45 minutes total
Get-Roasted Garlic Tahini Nut Sauce *25 minutes
6C8 cloves garlic
1 cup cashew butter
cup tahini paste
cup rice wine vinegar
cup lime juice
tsp white pepper
tsp salt
tsp tarragon
cup Midnight Oil
1) Heat oven to 350. Put garlic in an oven-safe pan,
coat with 1 tsp Midnight Oil and bake until golden
brown, 15C20 minutes. Mash when cool enough.
2) Spin up some of those silly sticks, and set fire to
your senses.
3) In a small saucepan, add remaining ingredients, and
bring to a simmer while whisking vigorously. If it needs
to be thinned out, just add a few tablespoons of
water. Once it reaches the right consistency, remove
from heat.
4) Drizzle kabobs (see next page) with nut sauce and
garnish with parsley.
