Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Sweet n Twisted Veggie Stir-Fry
with Sweet n Twisted Sauce
*40 minutes total
Sweet n Twisted Sauce *10 minutes
1 cup pineapple juice
cup tomato juice
cup ketchup
cup brown sugar
cup Midnight Oil
cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp vinegar
1 Tbsp Chinese 5 Spice (available at Asian
markets or on the Internet)
3 Tbsp cornstarch
cup water
1) Combine everything except cornstarch and water in
a saucepan and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes.
2) Mix cornstarch and water, then whisk into simmer-
ing sauce. Cook for 2 minutes.
3) Serve hot with rice.
Hint: If you want to store this tempting sauce, allow
to cool and put in airtight container. I like to have
some in the freezer for spur-of-the-moment ethnic
munchies.
