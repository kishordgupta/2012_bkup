Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Materials needed:
adoubleboiler(orapotthatallowsforastainlesssteel
bowl/pot to fit snugly on top)
water
4cupsbutter
weed(seechart,pg9)
coffeefilter
containerforstraining
freezerbags
1) Add water to bottom pot, then put on low heat. Put bowl or
second pot on top; melt butter.
2) Add weed, making sure that all leaf is submerged. Cover. Cook
for at least 30 minutes, up to 22 hours.
Note: You never want the butter to boil; that turns the butter
bitter and kills the THC. A safe temperature is below 230 Fahren-
heit. There is some debate on whether or not the longer cooking
process makes a difference to the strength of the oil. I have tried it
both ways, and believe that the longer you cook it, the better it is.
3) When cooking is done, pour liquid through the coffee filter, us-
ing a container to catch the fluid. (Save plant matter for a batch of
Pot-sto. See page 25)
4) Put the container into the freezer until fat hardens, about an
hour. Take out of the freezer and remove all the fat that has risen
to the top. That fat has now become the prize of any freezer:
Butter-Ganj.
5) Put Butter-Ganj into an airtight container and store in the
freezer.
