Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Pack the pipe and prepare for proper pampering.
2) Heat griddle or skillet over medium heat.
3) Combine all dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
4) Whisk in wet stuff, and try to get out all the lumps.
5) Coat skillet with oil, and ladle on batter. When little
bubbles mostly cover the top of the pancake, then its
probably time to flip itabout 2C4 minutes per side.
6) As soon as they come off the griddle, butter those
babies up and top them with whatever your taste buds
inspire.
Hint: This recipe can be modified for waffles by
adding another tsp baking soda and taking away
cup milk.
