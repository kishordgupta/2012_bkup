Fried Nice Rice
*40 minutes
2 cups white rice
4 cups water
tsp salt
cup soy sauce
cup Midnight Oil
1 bunch green onions, sliced diagonally
2 cups frozen peas
1 cup carrots, grated
1 tsp garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste
1) Put rice, water, and salt into a medium saucepan.
Bring to a boil; then reduce heat to low and cook for
25C30 minutes, or until all the water is gone and rice
is tender. Let cool.
2) This is a good time to get simmered with the bub-
bler.
3) Heat Midnight Oil in wok until smoking. Add garlic
and carrots; cook for 1 minute.
4) Add cooked and cooled rice, soy sauce, peas, green
onions, and seasonings.
5) Mix until consistent and heated all the way through.
6) Serve hot with green onion strings.
Hint: Rice stays fluffy if it s allowed to cool com-
pletely before cooking in the wok, but if you cant
wait, I understand.
