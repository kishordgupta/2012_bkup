Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
What are the portion sizes, and how long will it take to
kick in?
Well, heres the trickeveryone is different, and everyone re-
sponds differently. All of the recipes in this book use anywhere
from 1/2 cup to 2/3 cup of Butter-Ganj or Midnight Oil. I have
found that amount will lite up four to five people. If you are a
lightweight, start off with small portions, and wait to see how you
feel in 45 minutes to an hour.
What is the difference between using leaf or Bud?
Most of the THC is in the crystal that forms on the leaves. As the
bud appears, it gathers more crystal, therefore making the bud
three to six times more potent than the outer leaves.
What do I do about the killer munchies that attack 2 -3
hour after eating?
Do not eat more pot food! Always have plenty of non-fun food
around. If you try to eat more fun food when you get the munch-
ies, you might end up in a coma on the couch.
How can I make these recipes vegan?
Midnight Oil can replace the Butter-Ganj in most recipes. Swap soy
milk for milk. Xanthan gum can be used as an egg substitute.
Can I make these recipes without the magic ingredient?
Absolutely, yes. If you use plain butter or oil, the recipe will still
be delicious, just without the added side effects.
Will the THC vaporize out of my treats?
THC will vaporize at 375 degrees, so I have adjusted the recipes to
reflect this. Keep away from high heat to not burn away your buzz.
