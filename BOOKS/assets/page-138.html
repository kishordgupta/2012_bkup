Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Preheat your oven to 325.
2) After your first cup of morning beverage, grab your
favorite smoking tool, and step through the first gate
of Sunday bliss.
3) Now put all the dry ingredients in a bowl. Cut But-
ter-Ganj into the flour until butter chunks are pea-
sized. (Isnt it a cool color? I do love green.)
4) Add buttermilk; stir until it forms a soft dough.
5) This is the part where you can have variations on
what the biscuits look like. You could just spoon out the
dough; or you can add a bit more flour and roll out the
dough on a well-floured surface, then cut them into
any shape you like.
6) Place biscuits on a greased cookie sheet. Bake at
325 for 12C15 minutes.
7) Serve piping hot with more Butter-Ganj and Stoner
Syrup, jubilantly jumping into the second gate of al-
tered reality.
