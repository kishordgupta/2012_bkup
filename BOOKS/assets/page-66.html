Veg-Out Couscous Salad
*20 minutes
1 red pepper, finely diced
1 medium onion, finely diced
cup squash, finely diced
cup zucchini, finely diced
cup parsley, chopped
2 cup couscous, uncooked
cup Midnight Oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp marjoram
1 tsp sage
tsp lemon peel
cup lemon juice
4 cups water
Hint: This recipe is so fast and easy that you should
toss the magic green salad bowl at least 30 minutes
before starting, so you can be hungry.
1) Wash and cut veggies.
2) Put everything except the couscous in a large pot
and bring to a boil. Remove from heat.
3) Pour in couscous and cover. Let stand for 6C10
minutes, then fluff with fork.
4) Serve hot or cold; garnish with parsley sprigs.
