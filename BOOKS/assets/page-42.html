Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Cut up all the veggies.
2) Put everything in a large mixing bowl and give it a
good stir.
3) You must let the flavors get to know each other, so
try to wait at least 30 minutes.
4) Distract yourself by busting out your three-footer
(you know we all have one for special occasions), and
start the fiesta with your amigos.
5) Serve cold with corn chips.
