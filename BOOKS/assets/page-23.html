Crockpot Method
Hint: This method works on any type of oil . I like
having a stash of Midnight peanut oil , some
Midnight olive oil , and then a larger batch of Deep
Forest frying oil . Each has its specific purpose in
flavor, texture, and cooking temperature.
Materials needed:
crockpot
2cupsoil(yourchoicepeanut,olive,canola,vegetable,
etc.)
weed(seechart,pg9)
coffeefilters
funnel
glassjarwithanairtightlid
1) Pour oil into the crockpot, add weed, submerge, and cover. Turn
on low heat, and cook for 12 to 22 hours. Allow to cool before
proceeding.
2) Put the coffee filter into the funnel, the funnel in the jar, and
pour the oil through the filter.
3) Close the jar and keep in a cool, dark space.
