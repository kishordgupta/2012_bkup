Chronic Chicken Curry Salad
*40 minutes
3 lb boneless chicken breast
2 stalks celery, chopped small
cup carrots, grated
1 cup raisins
1 cup mayo
cup Midnight Oil
2 Tbsp garlic, minced
2 Tbsp curry powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2 Tbsp parsley flakes
1) Put chicken in a pot and cover with water. Boil for
20C25 minutes. Remove from water and allow chicken
to cool on a flat surface.
2) Dice celery, grate carrots, mince garlic, and put all
in large mixing bowl.
3) Speaking of bowls, this might be a good time to bust
out the chronic and lighten up.
4) When chicken is cool enough not to burn those deli-
cate fingers, dice it up, and dump it into the mixing
bowl.
