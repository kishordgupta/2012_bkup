Smoked-Out Chili Chutney
*15 minutes
cup golden raisins
cup currants
cup dried apricots
cup rice vinegar
cup Midnight Oil
tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
tsp celery seed
tsp coriander
tsp salt
tsp cayenne pepper
1) Grab the bong, gather your friends, and walk into a
small closet. Nobody is allowed to leave until they have
stoner x-ray vision.
2) Combine Midnight Oil and spices in medium sauce-
pan and bring to a simmer.
3) Add dried fruit, apple cider vinegar, and honey.
Continue to cook until liquid is gone, 10C15 minutes.
4) Allow to cool, and store for at least 24 hours.
5) Serve at room temperature with bread, crackers, or
whatever.
