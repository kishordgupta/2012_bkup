Toka-nut Chicken Curry
*35 minutes
2 lb boneless chicken
cup Midnight Oil
2 oz green curry paste
4 oz red curry paste
16 oz coconut milk
1 whole coconut
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander
1 tsp cardamom
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp ground clove
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp garlic powder
cup cashew pieces
1) I challenge you to make a bong out of a coconut
shell, and toke that nut.
2) Heat Midnight Oil in large sauce pan; add all spices
and pastes. Simmer for 3C4 minutes.
3) Add chicken and cook for 7C10 minutes. (Looks
good, doesnt it?)
4) Now to throw it over the top, add coconut milk and
simmer until sauce thickens, 15C20 minutes.
5) Serve hot over rice; garnish with cashews.
Hint: If curry doesnt thicken quickly enough , make
a slurry of 1 oz water and 3 Tbsp cornstarch , and
then whisk into sauce.
