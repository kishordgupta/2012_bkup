Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Apple filling *15 minutes
2 lb apples
cup Butter-Ganj
cup brown sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
tsp nutmeg
1) Cut apples in quarters and remove the core; cut
quarters into -inch thick slices.
Note: The thicker you slice the apples, the longer they
will take to cook.
2) Melt butter in a large skillet, add apples, sugar, and
spices.
3) Cook on low heat until soft, 7¨C10 minutes.
4) Remove from heat.
Hint: This could be a fruity snack if you skip the
crust.
