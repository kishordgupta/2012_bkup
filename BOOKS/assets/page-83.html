Jolly Green Bean Casserole
*30 minutes
32 oz frozen green beans
4 cups water
10 oz can cream of mushroom soup
10 oz milk
cup Midnight Oil
1 tsp garlic powder
tsp black pepper
24 oz can of onion strings
1) Put the water and green beans in a saucepan and
cook for 8C10 minutes.
2) Drain off water; add everything except half a can of
the onion strings. Give it a good stir.
3) Pour green beans into a 9x13 baking pan and bake
at 325 for 20 minutes.
4) Twist up a fat green stalk, and see how high you
get.
5) Remove from oven and add the other half of the
onion strings; bake for another 10 minutes.
6) Serve this heavenly treat hot, but dont burn your
tongueI know its hard to wait.
