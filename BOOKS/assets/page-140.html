Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Whisk together eggs and salt; then add flour, milk,
and Midnight Oil. Continue whisking until smooth.
2) Cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.
3) Use your time wisely: find a small closet and get
boxed.
4) Heat non-stick skillet until very hot. Grease skil-
let with oil, ladle in cup batter. Make sure that the
entire surface of the skillet is covered. It should make
a thin layer. Cook 2 minutes per side, using a rubber
spatula to pull it away from the pan and flip. (This is
where you get to show off to your friends.)
5) Repeat process until batter is gone.
