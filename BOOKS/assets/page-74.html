Veggie Kabobs *20 minutes
1 large yellow squash, cut in -inch half-moons
1 large zucchini, cut in -inch half-moons
1 medium red onion, cut in 1-inch cubes
8 oz button mushrooms
1 large red pepper, diced 1 inch
1 cup Drunk and Hi Vinaigrette (see page 24)
1 pack bamboo skewers
Hint: There are many ways to cook these yummy
treats, such as over an open fire, on your grill , in
the oven , or anything that gives it a good sear,
which brings out the essence of the vegetables.
1) Wash and process veggies, then shower them with 1
cup Drunk and Hi Vinaigrette.
2) Take a skewer and stab veggies however you want
them.
3) Heat cooking method to really hot and sear kabobs
on all sides, 3C5 minutes each side.
Hint: 2-3 sticks of kabobs with 3-4
0z of sauce should do the trick.
