Ganja-Whip *5 minutes
2 cups heavy whipping cream at room
temperature
cup powered sugar
1 oz jungle juice
1) Put heavy cream in mixing bowl. Use an electric
mixer, and whisk on high til it doubles in size.
2) Add powdered sugar and jungle juice.
3) Continue to whisk on high speed until whipped
cream has stiff peaks or when it mostly holds its shape.
4) Serve on whateveror whomeverever you want.
