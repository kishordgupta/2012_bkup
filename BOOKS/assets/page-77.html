Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Stoner Nip Syrup *20 minutes
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
1C2 oz jungle juice
1) Combine water and sugar in saucepan and place on
high heat.
2) Simmer until it reduces by two-thirds; this takes
about 15C20 minutes. It should resemble a thin syrup.
3) Add jungle juice and watch sugar turn into pure
green magic.
4) Pour over warm cake, and take a dip in the pool of
ganja forgiveness.
Hint: This syrup so easy to make that everyone who
owns this book should have it in the fridge, no mat-
ter what. It turns coffee, tea, lemonade, and what-
ever else you want to be sweet into an awesome body
buzz. And it keeps forever.
