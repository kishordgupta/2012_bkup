Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Heat Midnight Oil in medium pan.
2) Add flour, and mix until it becomes a paste. This
stage looks really cool, especially when you have in-
duced sparkly vision.
3) Continue cooking this cool-looking paste for 5 min-
utes, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
4) This is when you need a friend. Slowly add the milk,
whisking continuously. When your arm is ready to fall
off, ask your friend to take over and keep whisking un-
til the liquid is not lumpy.
5) Bring to a simmer and continue cooking for 7C10
minutes.
6) Serve over steak, and watch your reality shift.
