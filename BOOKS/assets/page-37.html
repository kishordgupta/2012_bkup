Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Note: This dish is not for the one-hitter quitter, so
step up to the challenge, and prepare to know the per-
fect munchie. Also, dont cook those mushrooms! heat
destroys psilocybin , the psychoactive compound.
1) Preheat the oven to 325.
2) Rehydrate the magic mushrooms with a little warm
water for 10 minutes. Then you have some groovy tea
to wash down your pizza.
3) Saut oil, garlic and shrimp until shrimp is bright
red and fully cooked, 3C5 minutes, then set aside.
4) Spread Pot-sto evenly over crust. (Be careful with
the Pot-stomore then 2 oz could leave you drooling
on the couch, see pg 25 for recipe.)
5) Add cheese and shrimp.
6) Bake at 325 for 14-17 minutes.
7) Add your magic mushrooms on top.
8) Slice and serve.
