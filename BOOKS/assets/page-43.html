Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Take Flight Spanish Rice
*45 minutes
cup Midnight Oil
1 medium onion, chopped small
1 small red pepper, chopped small
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups rice
8 oz can of tomato sauce
24 oz water
1 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp salt
1) Roll up on an international pocket rocket, buckle
up, and get lifted.
2) In medium skillet, heat Midnight Oil; saut garlic,
onion, and red pepper for 2 minutes.
3) Add rice and saut until light tan, 2C3 minutes.
4) Add tomato sauce, water, and seasonings.
5) Stir only once (OK, maybe twice); lower to a sim-
mer and cover.
6) Simmer until all liquid has cooked out, 30C40 min-
utes or until rice is tender. Resist the urge to stir it
during this process because it will make your rice a
sticky glob of goo.
7) Serve hot with cilantro leaves.
