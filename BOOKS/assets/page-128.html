Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Preheat oven to 325.
2) Combine flour and salt, add both butters, and cut
into flour until pieces are the size of small peas.
3) Add water. Using clean hands, form dough into a
ball, trying to work it as little as possible.
4) Allow dough to rest.
5) Now is the time to create an all-American smoke-
out, and spark up your fireworks.
6) Roll out dough to -inch thick. Youll need two cir-
cles at least 2 inches bigger than the pie pan. Cut the
circles; place one in ungreased pan. Use a fork to poke
holes in the crust, and bake at 325 for 8C9 minutes.
Remove from oven.
7) Fill with apple filling.
8) Place the second crust on the top, and crimp the
edges by pressing crust between your index finger and
thumb. Go all the way around the pie.
9) Cut four small vents in top.
10) Place on cookie sheet; and bake at 325, about
30C35 minutes or until golden brown.
