Blast-Off Croutons
*35 minutes
1 loaf of day-old bread
cup Midnight Oil
1C2 tsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp salt
tsp pepper
1) Preheat your oven to 300.
2) Cut bread into 1-inch cubes and put in a bowl.
3) Add salt, pepper, garlic, herbs, and Midnight Oil;
toss until evenly coated.
4) Place croutons on ungreased cookie sheet; bake at
300 for 20C25 minutes or until perfectly crunchy.
5) Allow to cool completely. Store in a Ziploc bag to
protect these crunchy morsels from moisture.
Hint: One of the awesome things about these crou-
tons is they never go bad. Even if they get a little
soft, theyll still do the trick.
