Rasta Mans Green Banana and
Potato Curry
*45 minutes
4 medium green bananas, sliced 1-inch thick
3 medium potatoes, 1-inch slices
cup Midnight Oil
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp garam masala (Indian spice)
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 cup veggie stock
1) Roll up a spliff, throw on some reggae, and trans-
port yourself to the islands. Dont bother smoking the
banana peels; it doesnt really work.
2) Peel bananas and potatoes, and cut into 1-inch
slices. Boil in salted water until tender, about 15C20
minutes. Drain off water and set aside. (OK, the hard
part is done.)
3) In medium saucepan, heat Midnight Oil and all spic-
es. Simmer for 2C4 minutes.
4) Add cooked bananas, potatoes, and veggie stock. Al-
low to simmer until sauce thickens, about 15C20 min-
utes.
5) Serve hot over basmati rice.
