Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Rinse potatoes and put into large pot. Cover with
water. Boil for 20C30 minutes.
Note: When a knife can pierce a potato with ease, its
done.
2) Drain potatoes and allow to cool on flat surface. (If
you are in a hurry, you could put potatoes on a cookie
sheet and put them into the freezer for 15C20 min-
utes.)
3) While you are in the freezer, grab some ice, pop it
in the three-footer, and chill out with your spuds.
4) When properly chilled, use a butter knife to peel po-
tatoes. The skins should come right off, or you could go
rustic-style and leave the skins on.
5) Cut potatoes into bite-sized pieces and place in
large mixing bowl.
6) Dice celery, onions, and pickles, and add to pota-
toes.
7) Add remaining ingredients. With clean hands, give it
a bit of a squish while still leaving it mostly chunky.
8) Dont forget to lick those fingers to wet that appe-
tite.
9) Serve chilled; garnish with dill sprigs.
