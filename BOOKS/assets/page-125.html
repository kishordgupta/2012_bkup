Smokem Horns BBQ Chicken
*1 hour
2 lb chicken pieces (use whichever piece is your
favorite, but consider that the more bone in the
piece, the longer it will take it to cook)
32 oz ketchup
cup red wine vinegar
cup Midnight Oil
cup water
tsp hickory-smoke flavoring
1 Tbsp molasses
1 tsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp dried onion
1 tsp ground chipotle
2 tsp tarragon
1 tsp cayenne pepper
tsp salt
1) Put everything but the chicken into a saucepan,
bring to a simmer, and cook for 3C5 minutes.
2) Get the cooking method nice and hot. (I prefer to
cook over a wood fire.) Slather the sauce all over that
delightful bird, and cook for 15C20 minutes on each
side.
