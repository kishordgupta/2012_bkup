Tomato Bud-chetta with Crustonies
*15 minutes
2 lb tomatoes, diced
1 medium red onion, finely diced
cup Midnight Oil
1 bunch fresh basil
1 lb mozzarella, diced in small pieces
3C5 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
cup lemon juice
1 baguette
1) Preheat the oven to 325.
2) Slice baguette -inch thick. (Dont cut slices too
thick or they wont get crunchy.) Lay slices flat on a
cookie sheet.
3) Lightly brush tops with Midnight Oil.
4) Bake and get baked at 325 for 8C12 minutes. Al-
low yourselfand the crustoniesto cool.
5) Put the diced tomatoes, onions, and mozzarella in a bowl.
6) Playfully chop the basil and mince the garlic, mak-
ing sure to watch those fingers!
7) Heres the fun part: Add the Midnight Oil, lemon
juice, and seasonings. Then toss that baby!
8) Serve the bud-chetta cold, surrounded by crunchy
crustonies. Garnish with basil leaves.
