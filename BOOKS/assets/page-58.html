Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Place eggs in large pot and fill with water to 1 inch
above eggs. Add salt; boil for 10C12 minutes.
2) Allow yourself to be seduced by the devil weed.
Whatever happens after that, just say, The devil
made me do it.
3) Remove pot of eggs from stove and run under cold
water for 5 minutes. (Its kind of fun to stir the eggs
with your hand during this process.) Allow eggs to cool
completely before peeling. Once cooled, carefully peel
eggs, trying to keep the whites intact. If this step turns
into a complete failure you can always make egg salad,
so dont sweat it.
4) Cut eggs in half, lengthwise. Remove yolks; place in
a separate bowl.
5) Rinse white halves and allow them to dry on a pa-
per towel. (You can skip this step if you dont care
what they look like.)
6) Add remaining ingredients to the bowl with egg
yolks and mix until smooth and creamy.
7) Take dried egg halves and fill with egg yolk mixture,
using a teaspoon or pastry bag.
8) Serve chilled. Garnish with sprigs of dill and dust
with paprika.
