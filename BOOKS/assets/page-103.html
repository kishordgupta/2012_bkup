Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Tea Party Almond Cookies
*45 minutes
2 cups flour
tsp baking powder
tsp baking soda
tsp salt
cup butter/margarine
cup Midnight Oil
cup sugar
1 egg
1 Tbsp almond extract
lb whole almonds (1 per cookie)
1) Preheat your oven to 325.
1) In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking pow-
der, baking soda, and salt.
2) In another bowl, mix the butter, Midnight Oil, and
sugar until its light and fluffy. Then add the egg and
almond extract.
3) Add flour mixture, then mix it all up. Use your
fingers to form the lump into two logs, each a little less
than a foot long. (Dont worry about it being all crumbly;
thats just its nature.) Wrap the logs in plastic wrap and
refrigerate for 30 minutes.
4) Now its tea-party time. Gather your green friends,
drink some tea, and properly prepare for yummy treats.
