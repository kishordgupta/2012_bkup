Wacky Tabacky Summer Veggies
*30 minutes
1 cucumber, thinly sliced
2 medium yellow squash, -inch slices
2 medium zucchini, -inch slices
1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup snow peas
cup carrots, grated
cup apple cider vinegar
cup Midnight Oil
cup sugar
1 bunch basil, chopped
1C2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
tsp cayenne pepper
1) Slice and dice your veggies; put them into a large
bowl.
2) Add the remaining ingredients, and toss it all up.
3) Refrigerate for one hour (or at least fifteen min-
utes). The flavors need to mingle and so do you.
4) Go out to the wacky-tabacky garden for some well-
deserved relaxation with your buds.
5) Serve veggies garnished with basil leaves.
