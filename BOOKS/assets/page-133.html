Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Charmed Cherry Almond Coffee Cake
*1 hour
Cake
cup Butter-Ganj
cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp almond extract
cup milk
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
tsp salt
1) Mix Butter-Ganj, sugar, almond extract, and egg
until creamy.
2) Mix in half of the dry ingredients, then add milk,
then the rest of the dry ingredients.
3) Pour into greased cake pan.
Topping
1 can cherry pie filling
cup brown sugar
3 Tbsp flour
1 tsp cinnamon
cup sliced almonds
3 Tbsp Butter-Ganj
