Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Mara-Nara-Wanna Sauce *40 minutes
1 medium onion, diced
1 small green pepper, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
cup Midnight Oil
16 oz can tomato sauce
16 oz can diced tomatoes
2 tsp fresh basil
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp fresh thyme
1 tsp tarragon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1) Saut onion, garlic, and green pepper in Midnight
Oil until soft.
2) Chop fresh herbs, add remaining ingredients, and
simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.
3) Stimulate your appetite with several deep inhala-
tions of laughing grass.
4) Serve hot, or put into a jar and refrigerate.
Hint: The uses for this sauce are limitless. Here are
just a few: lasagna, pizza, breadsticks, potzarella
sticks, pasta, and sandwiches.
