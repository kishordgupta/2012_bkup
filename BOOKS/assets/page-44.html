Hippie Jicama Slaw
*15 minutes
1 lb jicama [hic-a-ma], cut julienne-style (you
could use apples if you cant find jicama)
1 lb cabbage, shredded
2 medium red peppers, cut into -inch strips
cup Midnight Oil
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
1 lemon, juiced
1 lime, juiced
1 jalapeo, deseeded and minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp cumin
2 Tbsp sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1) Peel and julienne jicama (or apples) and red pep-
pers; shred cabbage. Set aside.
2) Now do what most hippies are good atget silly.
3) In medium bowl, combine lemon and lime juice,
garlic, sugar, and spices. Whisk for 2 minutes.
4) Add the veggies to the bowl and toss.
5) Serve chilled with cilantro leaves.
