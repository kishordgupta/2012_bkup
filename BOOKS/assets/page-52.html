Hazy Hummus with Pita
*15 minutes
3 cans garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
cup tahini paste
cup Midnight Oil
cup lemon juice
4C6 cloves garlic
1 tsp salt
1 tsp white pepper
2C3 Tbsp water
cup parsley
1 bag of pita bread
1) Place everything but the pita bread into your food
processor. (If you dont have a food processor, a blend-
er will work, but it wont be as smooth.)
2) Blend for 3C5 minutes or until thick and creamy.
Assure your friends the cure to munchies is on the way.
3) Slice that pita bread into 6 triangles. Lay them flat
on a cookie sheet and brush the tops with Midnight Oil.
Bake at 325 for 10C15 minutes.
4) Now bust out the hookah and get hazy.
5) Serve this dish hot or cold. Marvel at how fast and
easy it was.
