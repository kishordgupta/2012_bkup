Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
Hella-Hi-Ya Halava Cake with
Stoner Nip Syrup
*65 minutes total
Cake *45 minutes
2 cups almonds, ground
1 cups cream of wheat
2 eggs
1 cup milk
cup Butter-Ganj
cup butter
2 cups sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 tsp baking powder
1) Preheat your oven to 325.
2) This dessert must be blessed with kosher joint by the
rabbi herself.
3) Mix the butters and sugar together. Add eggs and
vanilla. Mix well.
4) Put cream of wheat and almonds in the food pro-
cessor and spin that baby up for 2C4 minutes or until
it looks like a coarse powder.
5) Add half of nut mixture to the green creamy-
dreamy butter.
6) Add milk and remaining nut powder. Lightly mix,
making sure its all wet.
7) Grease 9x13 baking pan and pour in batter.
8) Bake at 325 for 35C40 minutes.
