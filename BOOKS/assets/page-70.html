Lamb Chops with Twice-Blazed Mint
Glaze
*3 hour 50 minutes total
Lamb Chops *3 hours 30 minutes
2 racks of lamb chops
1 bunch of mint
1 cup apple cider vinegar
cup Midnight Oil
5 cloves of garlic, minced
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne
cup sugar
1) Chop half the mint (reserve the rest for garnish)
and mince the garlic. Combine all ingredients except
sugar in a container that will allow the racks of lamb
to be submerged and marinate for at least 3 hours.
(I know its hard to wait for marinating, but it really
does make the meat taste better.)
2) Take this time to marinate yourself.
3) When marinating is complete, preheat oven to 400.
4) Heat a skillet until smoking hot.
