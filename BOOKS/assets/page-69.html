Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
cup chopped mint
2 tsp garlic, minced
cup Midnight Oil
cup red wine vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp celery seed
cup sugar
1) Prepare to receive ancient wisdom from the goddess
through the mystical smoke of cannabis.
2) Slice cucumbers and onion, wedge tomatoes, mince
garlic, and chop mint.
3) Combine all ingredients into large mixing bowl and
pretend that you are that chef on Sesame Street.
(Dont forget to make that funny sound he made.)
4) Serve chilled, garnish with mint sprigs.
Hint: This dish gets better with age and keeps in the
cold box for almost two weeks.
