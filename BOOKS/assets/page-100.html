Ganjalicious: A Higher Level of Food
1) Heat Midnight Oil in medium saucepan. Add garlic,
and cook for 1 minute.
2) Add soy sauce, 2 cups water, cayenne pepper, and
honey. Simmer for 10 minutes.
3) Mix cup water and cup cornstarch until consis-
tency is smooth.
4) Whisk into soy sauce mixture, and cook for 5 min-
utes.
5) Add sesame seeds. (At this point, you could put this
sauce in a jar and refrigerate until you are ready to
use it.)
6) Place cooked chicken in large bowl. Pour two cups
of warm sauce over chicken, and toss until completely
coated.
7) Serve hot with rice.
